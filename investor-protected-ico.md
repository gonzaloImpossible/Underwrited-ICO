# Investor-Protected ICO
**Impossible Finance · Product Brief**

---

## What it is

A token sale structure where raised funds are held in escrow by Impossible Finance and released to the project team over 10 months — while investors retain a declining right to refund their investment for as long as they hold unclaimed tokens.

---

## The problem it solves

Standard ICOs transfer capital to the team on day one, leaving investors with no recourse if execution falls short. This creates rug risk, misaligned incentives, and reflexive sell pressure at TGE as early buyers rush to exit before others do.

---

## How it works

From TGE, investors have two mutually exclusive rights:

- **100% token claim** — available at any time post-TGE. Investor receives their full token allocation immediately and can trade freely. Exercising this permanently waives the refund right.
- **Declining refund right** — available for as long as the investor has not claimed their tokens. Starts at 100% of the invested amount and decreases by 10% each month as tokens are automatically airdropped. An investor who refunds returns their unclaimed token allocation and recovers the corresponding escrow balance.

Over 10 months, three things happen simultaneously each month:

1. Investors who have neither claimed nor refunded receive **10% of their purchased tokens** as an automatic airdrop — and their refund right decreases by 10%
2. **10% of raised funds** are released from escrow to the team
3. Investors who opted for a refund receive their **protected funds**

---

## The key invariant

> Escrow balance = Total outstanding refund rights — at all times.

The structure is solvent by design. Every investor who wants a refund can get one, regardless of how many others do simultaneously.

---

## Benefits

| Party | What they get |
|-------|--------------|
| **Investors** | Funded refund right up to 10 months post-TGE. Zero rug risk. Tokens fully liquid if claimed. |
| **Teams** | Larger raise potential, credibility signal, reduced TGE sell pressure, monthly accountability track record. |
| **Market** | Investors are incentivised to *not* dump at TGE — claiming tokens forfeits the refund right. Structurally healthier launches. |

---

## Why TGE sell pressure drops

Selling tokens at TGE requires claiming them first — which permanently waives the refund right. Rational investors hold rather than forfeit downside protection for marginal early exit gains. Supply pressure at launch is reduced by economic incentive, not lock-up contracts.
